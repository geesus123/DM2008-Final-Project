The Official Opening of the NTU Programming for Interaction Club

By Piglet  -  April 7 2023  -  8 mins read



The NTU Programming for Interaction Club is proud to announce its official opening. As a club dedicated to promoting and advancing the skills and knowledge of programming for interaction, we aim to bring together students with a passion for programming and provide them with the platform to showcase their skills and learn from each other.

What is the Programming for Interaction Club?

The Programming for Interaction Club is a student-led organization at NTU that aims to promote and educate students about the field of programming for interaction. Our club provides members with opportunities to learn, network, and create projects related to programming for interaction, such as interactive web applications, mobile applications, and more.

Why Join the Programming for Interaction Club?

The Programming for Interaction Club offers a unique opportunity for students to develop their programming skills and gain practical experience in the field of interaction design. By joining our club, members can participate in workshops, hackathons, and other events, where they can work with like-minded individuals to create innovative and interactive projects.

Our Future Plans

The Programming for Interaction Club is committed to providing a valuable experience for its members. In the future, we plan to expand our club's offerings by introducing more workshops and events to help members develop their skills and knowledge. We also aim to collaborate with other organizations to provide members with more opportunities to showcase their work and gain exposure to the industry.

Come join us!

The NTU Programming for Interaction Club is excited to begin its journey and looks forward to welcoming new members. By joining our club, students can develop their programming skills, network with other like-minded individuals, and gain practical experience in the field of interaction design. We invite students to join our community and be part of the exciting world of programming for interaction.
