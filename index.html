SSIA-NTU Student Branch

ABOUT US

The Singapore Semiconductor Industry Association (SSIA) is a non-profit organization that represents the semiconductor industry in Singapore. The SSIA-NTU Student Branch (SSIA-NTU SB) is a student-led organization that is affiliated with SSIA, and is dedicated to promoting the interests of students who are interested in the semiconductor industry.

Our mission is to provide students with opportunities to learn about the latest developments in the semiconductor industry, and to network with industry professionals and like-minded peers. We organize various events and activities, such as talks, and workshops, to help students gain a better understanding of the industry and develop the skills that are required for a successful career in the semiconductor industry.
